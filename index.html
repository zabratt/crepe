well first : what s crepe ?

A crêpe or crepe is a dish made from unleavened batter or dough that is cooked on a frying pan or a griddle. Crêpes are usually one of two varieties: sweet crêpes or savoury galettes. They are often served with a wide variety of fillings such as cheese, fruit, vegetables, meats, and a variety of spreads

    what u need (ingredients)

  • 2 large egges
  • half cup of milk
  • half of a cup of water
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter melted
  1. this will take 15 munites from your time

To make delicious crepes, begin by combining half a cup of milk, half a cup of water, and one cup of all-purpose flour in a mixing bowl. Whisk these ingredients together until the batter is smooth and free of lumps. Next, add two tablespoons of melted butter to the mixture, stirring well to incorporate. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with a bit of butter or oil. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan, tilting it to spread the batter evenly and thinly across the surface. Cook the crepe for about one to two minutes, or until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown. Flip the crepe and cook for another minute. Repeat this process with the remaining batter. Serve your crepes warm with your favorite fillings or toppings.
